About our collaboration

ScriptCo and Managed Health are thrilled to announce an innovative collaboration aimed at transforming the healthcare landscape, making prescription medications more affordable and accessible for all. Our shared vision and complementary strengths are paving the way for a healthier and cost-effective future.

At Managed Health, we're on a mission to simplify and revolutionize healthcare. We recognize the complexities, under-utilization, and high costs that often burden the healthcare system. Our unique approach focuses on health management, improving employee engagement, and delivering the highest quality healthcare at no out-of-pocket cost to individuals and organizations alike.
